Welcome to YOLO Object Detection!

This application leverages advanced object detection technology to identify and differentiate between vehicles and humans in images. Using a state-of-the-art YOLO (You Only Look Once) model, it can accurately analyze and annotate your images, highlighting detected vehicles and people. Simply upload your image, and let our tool provide real-time insights and visual annotations. Perfect for applications in security, traffic monitoring, and more.
